Korean Business Competition

Speaker

Korea Business Competition had 350 participants from a minimum of 20 top-level international schools participating in the year 2020. Through presentations and Q&A sessions, judges choose the most ideal business to address the prompt.

To win this award, I dedicated four hours each week for ten weeks. Answering the prompt of a business promoting health and well-being, our team came up with the idea of creating a food planning app that uses genetic analysis to create a diet plan fit for each individual. I was in charge of planning the marketing strategies and estimating finance.
