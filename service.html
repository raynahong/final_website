Freedom Speakers International Global High School Union

FSI-GHSU IT Manager

The FSI-GHSU is an organization of HS students from more than thirty different high schools located globally; members provide private English tutoring sessions and coordinate Unity Musical Concerts to help and fundraise for North Korean Refugees.

FSI-GHSU faced many challenges in keeping everyone participating together during the pandemic because it was difficult to communicate with more than 200 members at the same time. So as the IT manager of FSI-GHSU, I created an app that allows quick matching of English tutors to North Korean Refugees and a direct messaging function to communicate.

Conrad Challenge Innovators

President/Leader

The distinction of Conrad Innovator denotes the students who have excelled in the competition by submitting a noteworthy Business Plan, exceeding all the requirements for semi-finals. Out of 950 teams from 45 countries, only 250 teams were named innovators.

To win this award, I led team H2ohoo, to come up with an alternative for the plastic cosmetic package using usage of sodium alginate and calcium chloride.

I was also a part of Team CassAfast, and came up with a new GM Cassava to solve hunger in South Africa. Both teams were recognized as candidates for the finals.
